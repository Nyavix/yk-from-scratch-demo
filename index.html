Our story

A family, baking from scratch.

Cong trained as a professional baker in Vietnam. He came to Yellowknife in 2021 — worked at Subway, then Barren Ground Coffee, then opened From Scratch with his family.

On opening day, they sold out by 3pm. They've kept it up since — Japanese breads, Vietnamese banh mì, croissants and boba, all made from scratch, every morning.
