Our story

From Vietnam to Yellowknife — from scratch.

Cong Thanh Huynh trained as a professional baker in Vietnam and came to Yellowknife in 2021 with flour in his blood and a plan. He worked, he learned the North, and in April 2024, he and his wife Ann opened From Scratch in Centre Square Mall.

They sold out before 3pm on day one. The neighbours came back the next morning. They've been coming back ever since — for the shokupan, the banh mì, the boba, the matcha doughnuts that regulars call the best they've ever had.

None of it comes from a mix or a freezer. Every loaf, every roll, every bubble tea is made the way the name says: from scratch.

"We had a lot of support from everyone. I love it — I love the energy of everyone coming in." — Ann
est. 2024 · Yellowknife, NT
